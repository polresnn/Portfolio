I was born and raised in Mexico, and I'm currently in my third semester of Computer Science. I've always been drawn to the places where engineering meets craft, the technical side of things that most people never think about.

Most of my personal projects live at the intersection of code and precious materials. I'm interested in authenticity and security systems for luxury goods provenance, verification, the kind of digital infrastructure that protects value over time. That's the world I want to grow into.

Languages matter to me for the same reason. I'm currently learning Italian, and I plan to follow with Russian and Chinese, not just for the markets, but because I think understanding a culture's language changes how you understand what they value.
